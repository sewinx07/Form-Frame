# Form & Frame

**Luxury creative agency portfolio platform** — a full-stack Next.js application with a cinematic dark-mode frontend, role-based admin backend, interactive geometric background, and contact form with email notifications.

Form & Frame is a digital creative agency specializing in three disciplines:

- **Design** — UI/UX & Branding. Crafting digital interfaces and brand identities where every curve, color, and component is intentional.
- **Frame** — Cinema & Post-Production. Cinematic storytelling through frame-perfect editing, color grading, and motion design.
- **Code** — Next-Gen Development. Architecting performant, scalable applications with modern stacks.

The platform features a curated portfolio showcase, an admin backend for managing projects and site content, and a contact system that notifies the team via email when new inquiries arrive.
